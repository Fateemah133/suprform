About Hasjok Hospital

Welcome to HFM Hospital Since 1891, HFM Hospital has stood as a pillar of healthcare in the culturally diverse community of ota,ogun state. We are dedicated to providing compassionate care in a welcoming atmosphere. As a community hospital in the heart of ota, we provide primary and specialty care services when you need it most. We offer a wide range of high-quality services, supporting patients at every stage of life.

Whether you need emergency care, specialized treatments, or routine health services, HFM Hospital is here for you Discover how we care for our community and why we have been a trusted hospital Information in ota, ogun state, for well over a century.
